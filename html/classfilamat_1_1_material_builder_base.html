◆ Platform

enum Platform
strong

High-level hint that works in concert with TargetApi to determine the shader models (used to generate GLSL) and final output representations (spirv and/or text).

When generating the GLSL this is used to differentiate OpenGL from OpenGLES, it is also used to make some performance adjustments.
